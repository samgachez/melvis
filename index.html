About Me

People-First HR Leadership

I am a results-oriented Human Resource professional with over five years of progressive HR experience spanning healthcare, county government, and polytechnic sectors in Kenya.

My passion lies in building people-centred organizations — from digitizing HR operations and implementing HRMIS solutions to fostering inclusive workplace cultures through employee engagement initiatives.

Having served as an HR Officer at Premier Hospital Ltd and HR Assistant at Valley Hospital, I bring hands-on expertise in end-to-end HR management: payroll, recruitment, compliance, employee relations, and digital transformation.

I am equally grounded in leadership and student governance, having served as Secretary General of the Mount Kenya University Students' Association, Electoral Delegate, and Academic Secretary — experiences that sharpened my communication, negotiation, and strategic thinking skills.
